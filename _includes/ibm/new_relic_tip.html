If a PagerDuty incident is created by New Relic, Do not resolve the PagerDuty incident yourself! Monitoring will detect that the problem is fixed and automatically resolve the incident. If the problem is not fixed a new PagerDuty incident will not be opened. If you are not sure of the source of the incident, look at the PagerDuty details tab,if you see a URL like the follow, URL: https://alerts.newrelic.com/accounts/1926897/incidents/59674793, New Relic is the originator.

If an indicent is resolved and it keeps open for unsual period of time in PagerDuty, review New Relic Incidents, If you don't see the PagerDuty listed there, close it form PagerDuty and open a new entry at Ops-Platform-Onshift-Report
